Experience

Target Corporation

This past summer I joined the Enterprise Services team as an undergraduate intern at Target. I was exposed to a variety of the technologies the Enterprise Services team uses to build and manage all of Target's internal APIs, such as Github, Apigee, Chef, Jenkins and Cassandra. During my internship, I built a web application in Grails, Groovy on Rails, to analyze the potential profitability of expanding Target's partnership with Starbucks Coffee Company by building more Starbucks stores inside of Target stores across the U.S.
